Working Project: HaPlay Computer

We are currently working on an upcycling project called HaPlay Computer, which repurposes abandoned consumer electronics into devices with functions beyond their original state.

The electronic devices we handle include smartphones and STBs (Set-up Boxes), which are being redesigned into Writerdecks and Cyberdecks (handheld computers) to restore productivity and extend usage time.

If you're interested in our work, please visit haplaycomputer.com for more information.

HaPlay Writerdecks
