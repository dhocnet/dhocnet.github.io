ACTIVE PROJECT

We are currently working on a project that began in February 2025. This project, called HaPlay Computer, upcycles abandoned consumer electronics, specifically smartphones and STBs (Set-up Boxes).

We don't simply repair smartphones and STBs; we completely transform their basic functions from phones and TVs into handheld computers, Cyberdecks and Writerdecks.

If you're interested, you can visit our main website at haplaycomputer.com.

Below are the two main models we work on. You can see all available variants directly on their respective pages.

HaPlay Writerdecks

HaPlay Writerdeck is designed for writing needs with a minimalist design and special document processing software that is suitable for nomadic urban writers.

View all Writerdeck variants

HaPlay Cyberdecks

HaPlay Cyberdeck is specifically designed to be your computing companion on the go. It's ergonomically designed for ease of use on the go.

View all Cyberdeck variants
